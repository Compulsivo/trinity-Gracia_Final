Subclass Skill Certification:
You can be certified at levels 90, 91, 92 and 93. You can practice common skills at Level 90 and 91, common or special skills at Level 92, and ultimate skills at level 93.
Common skills are not occupation specific. They can be learned regardless of class. Special skills and ultimate skills are specific to your occupation.
The effects of skills learned through this certification can overlap each other, even if they are the same kind of skill.
